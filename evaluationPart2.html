Evaluating ROMULUS

Part 2: Evaluating ontological alignments

Look through the example explaining the process of evaluating an alignment.

Example Alignment:

Concept from Ontology 1 Concept from Ontology 2
Alignment x Chips Crisps
Your choice Agree       Partially Agree       Disagree       I don't know       Skip      

Meaning of options:

  • Agree: I agree that chips and crisps are exactly the same thing.
  • Partially agree: Chips and crisps are sometimes the same thing. However, if the chips we are talking about refers to french fries, this is different.
  • Disagree: Chips and crisps are two totally different things.
  • I don't know: After thinking about this alignment, I still do not know.
  • Skip: I don't wish to answer this.
